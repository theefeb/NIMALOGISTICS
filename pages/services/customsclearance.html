Documents & Compliance

We ensure all shipments meet the highest standards of international compliance. Here are the essential documents required:

Commercial Invoice

Includes item description, value, and transaction terms for customs clearance.

Air Waybill (AWB)

Acts as the contract of carriage and tracking document for your shipment.

Export Permit

Required by authorities to approve outbound shipment of certain goods.

Certificate of Origin

Specifies the country of manufacture for tariff or trade requirements.

Import License

Necessary for certain goods to enter the destination country legally.
